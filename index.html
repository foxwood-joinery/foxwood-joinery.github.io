About

Foxwood Joinery is a family run Company established in 2010.

Directors John and James along with their skilled and ever increasing workforce provide a high-quality joinery service for the construction industry.

We take pride in our work and are dedicated to completing projects within budget and in line with the schedules.We are based in the Northwest and have covered many other regions throughout the Country.

Services

Foxwood Joinery provide qualified CSCS joiners for a wide range of new build and refurbishment projects.
We can provide Labour Only, Labour and Fixings or Supply and Labour Prices.

Joist and Flooring

Trussed Roof Structures

Traditional Roof

Kitchens

Cladding

IPS Panel/Toilet Cubicles

We work alongside main contractors and architects from the start of the project to the finish.
We have an exceptional on and off site management team providing support throughout all projects undertaken.
